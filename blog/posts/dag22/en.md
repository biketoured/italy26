# The day in short
After a cool night next to lake Cicata, where I got to see a spaceship launch into space 🚀 (!), I rolled down towards the jonian coast. Lots of pastries for breakfast and stole a couple of oranges. This happened to be where the best lemon and oranges in Italy grow, and I could really taste it!!!<br>
The rest of the day was mostly about surviving the heat. I have a bandana round my forehead to avoid getting sunburnt and losing too much water, but the heat was relentless. The evening was spent on Rocca Impereale before finding a beach to pitch my tent along the coastline.
