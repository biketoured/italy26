# The day in short
After breaking my own personal lifetime record in elevation gain yesterday, today would become almost as much on a much shorter distance. 
This is because I'm biking the national park of Sila, a famous plateau of hills with lakes above 1000 meters above sea level. As I left Amato, whoch means loved in Italian, I eventually reached 800m plateau, and around lunchtime another heavy climb up to 1400m. Then I could enjoy some flatter roads before reaching Lorica, dubbed as the central town of the park. I enjoyed some late lunch and a nice lake view. <br>
The air up here is substantially softer and fresher than down along the coast. There number of warning signs for ice and snow told me this area can have much snow in the winter. They even have a bob pist 🛷, along with a cross country skiing centre! Pine forests and similar vegetation as we have in Skåne made me ponder if the climate here is actually similar to parts of southern Sweden? Who knows... All I can say is I needed some cooldown after a week of hot days in Sardinia and Sicily!<br>
Anyway, I pitched my tent by a lake where there are many others already. After seeing the sunset I also got to see a rocket launch into space far far away. Truly spectacular engineering!

## How did my body feel?
Feeling well rested and not too fatigued even though it's been 5000 meters of elevation the past two days.

## Best moment of the day
Riding up in the Sila plateau on relatively flat roads and moderate temperatures. Some nice downhill curves as well.

Light breakfast. Heavy and savoury lunch with cheese, meat and fries. Most like junk food-type of for I had so far, but I needed it!

## Landscape & nature
Very similar to Nordic forests and lots of pastures for cows. Italians consider Sila as a nice escape from the summer heat and I can really see why! I could however see some abandoned hotels so it was probably more popular historically. 

[photo:2]

## Smells & details
As mentioned I noticed similar climate and nature as Scandinavia. But there is generally more birds, insects and animals. More sun, more flora and fauna. I'm grateful I have ear plugs because the frogs and ducks were going crazy at dusk.

## Weather
Sunny, no clouds. 17-24°C but dropped to 18 at the higher altitudes. Nighttime in the tent was 11-14°C.

## How do I feel overall?
Happy to not feel like I'm overheating, but this night was actually rather cold.
