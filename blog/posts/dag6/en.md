## How did my body feel?
I feel fairly good after all. Raul has been a great host, he is wise and very interesting.

## Best moment of the day
Viewing the small villages of Cinque Terre from above. This is a truly spectacular region.

[photo:1]

## Person I met
Paul, an Andorran going on a long journey from Bordeaux to Bologna and beyond. It was nice to share the ride down from the panoramic road to La Spezia.

## What did I eat?
Paul and I had lunch, we got lots of grano padano cheese for the food, I had an absolutely delicious lasagna, and refueled energy with gummy bears on my way past the flat coast southwards.

## Landscape & nature
Very mountaious and steep coastline, I understand that Cinque Terre is avoided by bigger or older cars, I almost exhausted my legs in multiple climbs.
After La Spezia there was a vast plain past Massa and Carrara.

## Smells & details
The swallows collecting bugs over a stream at the border. The sunbeams coming through the clouds after a rainy day. The mist rising fron the foresty hills of Cinaue Terre. Great views. All the colourful houses were pretty to look at.

## How do I feel overall?
Fairly good but sometimes I wish I didn't have to plan so much when and where to eat. I'm always hungry when the restaurants are closed (~14-17) somehow :/
