# The day in short
The day started in Noto, a town famous for islta architecture. However, after an easy long morning the clock had already turned 10, and the thermometer showed 23. The sunrays were getting warmer every minute and I wanted nothing but to arrive in Siracusa before midday.<br>
The ride to Siracusa was easy and mellow, between lemon orchards and farms. Siracusa is a flowery, touristy city with beaches and boats everywhere. Somehow I missed the large Greek temple in the middle of the old town, but I had already seen one outside Agrigento.
After a quick haircut I tasted some local food. The waiter, who seemed to be the boss of the restaurant, was interested in my journey and we talked for a little while. He even gave me half a pizza and a cannolo with limoncello. That would definitely not happen if I could not speak Italian! <br>
Between Siracusa and Catania there was a massive hydrocarbon industrial complex. After a while I could see the enormous volcano materialize far far away in the distance. It was so much taller than I thought, at over 3300 masl it is the tallest mountain in Italy outside of the Alps. The evening was spent in Catania and I found a reasonable hotel. 
Travelling Italy in May is not only beneficial due to lower temperatures than the summer, but also because there is always available rooms in hotels and B&Bs.

## How did my body feel?
A little weaker than the previous days, probably because I had a light dinner yesterday.

## Best moment of the day
The heat was sapping energy all day, so finally getting a haircut and feeling the air brush my head again was probably the best feeling of the day!

A nice breakfast at the B&B. In Siracusa I ate Caponata with fish, olive salad, pizza, and finished it off with cannolo with limoncello for lunch. Some pistachio cornettos and juice. A nice kebab in Catania for dinner.

Honestly today was the worst day so far considering smells. The industries smelled like burnt plastic, fumes and gas. Lots of cars and mopeds without catalysts, and tons of trash along the road. But the city parks are awesome with large green trees and flowers!

The heat is demanding. I need some good sleep.
