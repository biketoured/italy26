## Person I met

## What did I eat?
I ate some snacks in Finale Ligure before heading off, then had a lunch with Stefano and an excellent pizza dinner with a cherry cake as dessert. Magnifico!

## Landscape & nature
The mountains reach out all the way to the coast, between the flat areas. When we climbed Sassello (famous for their Amaretti Morbidi) we reached foresty mountainous region with pine trees and untouched forests. Passa del Vaielli was breathtaking, with lots of flowers and green grass.

[photo:2]

## Smells & details
Along the coast there was salty air with fumy smells and flowery scents. The foresty smells in the inner oarts of Liguria were nice. Cloudy all day, good for my skin, it needs some recovery!

## How do I feel overall?
Probably need some recovery tomorrow morning before going. Also don't know yet if I'm taking the ferry from Genoa or Livorno. P.S: I took it from Livorno.
