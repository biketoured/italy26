# The day in short
Today would be 32 degrees. My very kind host of the B&B offered me carpooling to Cagliari. Since I didn't want to be late to the ferry, leaving once ever week, I accepted the proposition. The only booking I did today was to visit a large store of Nuraghe culture. 
It is truly eye-opening how these structures are still standing after 3000 years. He took me up to a spot where we could watch three town from above. We could see both flamingos and a sailing competition. As I lunched together with Manueles family in Cagliari we discovered two very unlikely things: <br>
First, I had met the brother of Manueles wife completely by chance the day before. We discovered this because they wanted to follow me on Instagram and there he was already following me. On an island of 1.6 million I had successfully met a brother and a sister on two completely different places and occasions!
Secondly, Manuele's daughter had lived in Skara for two weeks on a partnership between her school and Katedralskolan in Skara. (Neighbouring the municipality Götene where I grew up! Incredible coincidences!
Later I entered the ferry and bought a suite for 50 euro because I need some quality sleep.
