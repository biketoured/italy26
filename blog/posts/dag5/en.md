Liguria has been heavenly to bike, a region where beaches, cities and mountains are glued together. Maybe that's why I "missed" the ferry to Sardinia? Tuttavia, there is always an alternative and I have a feeling it's going to be even better than plan A. I will take a ferry from Livorno 200km away. I could make it there by tomorrow by bike + train. Post Scriptum: I actually decided to make a loop around Cinque Terre just to see some of the beautiful little towns the following day. So Liguria got me for the coming day too!


## How did my body feel?
Took an easy morning rolling down to Genoa. Legs still a bit shaky from yesterday's hilly challenge with Stefano.

## Best moment of the day
Being received at Osteria del Bai, where I was welcomed by Augostino and had a nice conversation. Thanks Ale!

## What did I eat?
