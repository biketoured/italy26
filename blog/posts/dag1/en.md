## Landscape & nature
Very hilly and picturesque. The French Riviera has really earned it's reputation, the views are terrific<em> and sometimes terrifying ;)</em> Flowers and lush foliage everywhere. Palm trees and sheared bushes ornament the luxury villas. Still, there is a fair bunch of neglected houses and trashy yards.

Still can't believe it hasn't even been a full day yet! Time to catch some sleep though.
