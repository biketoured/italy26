.
The day started with easy tailwind, rolling through the Sardinian hinterland. I had breakfast In Tempo Pausiano and a light lunch in Oschiri, where a nice woman helped me pick the best local pasticceria. The panadas were tasty!, but the dessert was even better!. Upon reaching Ozieri I took a siesta from the sun and indulged a 1kg tiramisu ice cream.
Thereafter 70km went like a breeze. The last 30km were beautiful along cork-tree pastures grazed by sheep and cows.

## How did my body feel?
Lactic acid in my legs, should stretch more .

## Best moment of the day
Meeting the host at the agricamp with a donkey upon arriving at the place!

## Person I met
Spoke with some locals. Spoke with a lady at the Nuraghe Majory site who taught me a lot about the Sardinian history, super interesting!

## What did I eat?
Panada do Oschiri, 1kg Tiramisu, some salami and goat cheese, and some bread.

## Landscape & nature
Rolling hills with soarse oak forests all over the area. Feels like a savanna sometimes.

## Smells & details
The air felt fresh and reinvigorating before midday, thereafter it was dey and suffocating.

## How do I feel overall?
Fairly good. A little cold because the days are so hot and the nights drop under 10°C.  Sleeping in a tend tonight as well.
