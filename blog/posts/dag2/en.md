A good night's rest at Guilhomme's maison was perfect for such an eventful day. Luckiky for me it was windy and not too warm. But carrying the bike up ~200 stairsteps in the afternoon sun was very demanding!

## Best moment of the day
Talking to several fantastic people along the way wishing me all the best.

## Person I met
The british ladies, the Swiss photographer, the Swedish man in Rouquebelle, the Monégasque man cleaning a yacht.
