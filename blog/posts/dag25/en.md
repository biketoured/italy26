# The day in short
The day started in a hostel in Brindisi. I got to know Eduardo, an Italian man with lots of interesting insights to share about the culture of Italy. We spent much time talking about how the Scandinavian and Mediterranean cultures differ.
The villages I went for this day were Ostuni, Alberobello, Locorotondo, Monopoli, and Polignano al Mare. All of them are stunning and tourist-heavy, especially Alberobello with its abundance of Trulli, the Apulian traditional circular houses.<br>
In one of these villages I met an Australian lady. The streets had just been transformed to streams by a flash of hail and rain, so I didn't mind talking for a while. Many people are busy and too occupied to have conversations, so I really enjoyed talking with Cheryl. The thing I like about travelling alone is that it's easier to find people who are willing to talk.
In the evening I found a B&B for an ok price in Polignano al Mare.

## Weather
Sunny, 18-27°C all day until 16, then hail and rain for 20min and thereafter it was cloudy mostly, with a lower temperature around 18.

## How did my body feel?
I felt like this day was light even though I did 130km and 1300m elevation. That's nice! But most of all I was relieved by the rain because I need some lower temperatures.

## Best moment of the day
Seeing the pretty villages and biking/walking around the narrow streets!
