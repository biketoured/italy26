// About me

Curious. Analytic. Adventurous.

Welcome to my site! I am a Swedish 24 year-old with great passion for sports and adventuring in new places, preferrably in combination. When I am not working or training I like to learn new stuff, for example learning a new language, reading something, or building this particular website!
Currently based in Skellefteå, Sweden, I spend my days analysing electricity markets and much of my spare time planning and preparing for this passion project: Bikepacking across Italy 2026.

My project Italy26 will cover 4,000km through Italy covering Sardinia, Sicily and the mainland.
My objective is to combine challenging efforts with profound encounters with Italian people and the Italian culture. This project has neither sponsors nor a predestined goal. It is purely driven by passion from deep within... and gelato!

Italia 2026 — 4,000 km

Starting in Nice, France, I'll ride along the Ligurian coast to Genoa, then island-hop through Sardinia and Sicily before snaking my way north via Calabria, Apulia, Campania, Abruzzo, Marche to Veneto and Lombardy.
